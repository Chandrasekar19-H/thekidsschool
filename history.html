Our FAQ’s

Frequently Asked
Questions About Psbb Kindargarten

"Have questions about our kindergarten? Find answers to the most common queries about our programs, safety, curriculum, and more, to help you make the best choice for your child."

  • 1. What age group do you accept?
    We welcome children from ages 2 to 6 years, providing age-appropriate activities for each stage.
  • 2. What are your school hours?
    Our regular hours are 8:30 AM – 3:30 PM, Monday to Friday, with optional extended care available.
  • 3. Do you provide meals and snacks?
    Yes! We provide healthy and balanced meals and snacks, and we can accommodate special dietary needs.
  • 4. Is your staff certified and trained?
    Yes! All our teachers and staff are fully certified, trained in early childhood education, and committed to providing a safe and nurturing environment.
  • 5. What’s your class size and ratio?
    We maintain small class sizes with a low student-to-teacher ratio to ensure each child gets personalized attention and support.
